Help Us Make Camp Infinity A Success For 260+ Underprivileged Kids

About Our Cause

This year, our family has grown. With more than 260+ children eager to embark on a journey of discovery and empowerment, we’re doubling our efforts to ensure that every child gets the chance to experience the magic of Camp Infinity. To accommodate this increased number, we’ll be organizing not one, but two camps – one dedicated to 152+ girls and another for 110+ boys.

Our commitment to providing a life-changing experience remains steadfast. Each child deserves the opportunity to explore, learn, and dream without limitations. For many of these children, Camp Infinity is not just a getaway; it’s a lifeline – a chance to break free from the confines of their circumstances and embrace a world of endless possibilities.

However, we cannot achieve this vision alone. We need your support, your generosity, and your belief in the power of transformation. The approximate cost for each child to participate in this 2-day, 1-night outing is ₹2000. Your donation will directly contribute to covering essential expenses such as accommodation, meals, transportation, and activities, ensuring that every child can fully immerse themselves in the Camp Infinity experience.

join us on this journey of compassion, solidarity, and empowerment. Your support can make all the difference in the lives of these incredible children. Together, let’s make Camp Infinity 2024 an unforgettable adventure, a transformative experience, and a beacon of hope for generations to come.

Thank you for being a part of our mission to create a brighter future for every child.
