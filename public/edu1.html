Help RAVI KUMAR Raise Funds For PGDM In DIHE, Greater Noida

About Our Cause

Hello, my name is Ravi Kumar and I come from a lower middle class family. I have an opportunity that is much than myself pursuing a Post Graduate Diploma in Management at Delhi. This is a once in a lifetime opportunity for me as the acceptance rate of this prestigious university in India, making it a dream for many.

My journey here has not been easy. My father, an labor, had to give up his education early on to take care of our family. Despite the financial challenges, he has always placed a high value on my education, saving every possible penny for it. My mother, a homemaker, has been battling a rare condition, Myositis with Muscular Dystrophy, for the past 7 years. She is now bedridden, and a significant part of our income goes towards her medical expenses. This situation has added to our financial burdens, making it a daily struggle to balance our dreams with our reality.

To support my family and finance my living expenses during my Bachelor’s without burdening them I served as a volunteer teacher to cover my living expenses and textbooks. Beyond these duties, I volunteered with an NGO, educating disabled children and embracing the joy of making a difference in their lives. Even though I've faced many challenges, I've managed to get this far because I worked hard and earned scholarships from High school till my Graduation. I feel confident I will be a successful student while studying in the Delhi because I have been very resilient throughout my college years. I'm hoping to further my studies at one of the top universities, but the financial side of things is quite daunting. The total cost for my PGDM is 8,77,376 rupees, which, unfortunately, is more than I can afford on my own. I'm currently working to secure 3 lakh rupees through loans, along with the support of my family, and friends. However, finding additional loans without collateral is a significant problem. I humbly request any assistance or guidance in navigating this challenge.

Financial Breakdown (June 2024 – May 2026) Tuition fees – INR 4,77,376 (First Sem- 96,188, Second Sem- 96,188, Third Sem- 1,42,500 & Forth Sem - 1,42,500) Hostel Fees – INR 3,64,000 (1,32,000 per annum) Living & other Expenses - INR 1,36,000 Total: INR 8,77,376

I invite you to be part of my journey "From Dreams to Reality." Your support can change the course of not just one life but also inspire a ripple effect of empowering a boy and making a change. Your support and kindness are appreciated during this extremely difficult time - Thank you from the bottom of our hearts. Any donation amount is very much appreciated! Thank you for your time and consideration. If you need any further clarifications, please get in touch with me at ravig8383@gmail.com or 99999-XXXXX.
